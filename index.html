About

Behind The Lens

Hi, I'm Matthew Skaza, a 17-year-old photographer based in Chicopee, Western Massachusetts. I specialize in sports photography while also capturing portraits and creative moments. With over two years of experience behind the camera, I focus on creating images that preserve meaningful moments.

As a student-athlete and football captain, I bring an athlete's perspective to sports photography by understanding the emotion, preparation, and intensity behind every moment. I graduate in 2027 and study Information Support Services and Networking in the Technical Education program at Chicopee Comprehensive High School.

I want to continue building my brand and my image as both a photographer and a student-athlete, so I'm excited to launch this website!

My pricing depends on the event and sport. I offer sports photos for $5 for 5 photos or $7–$15 for the full gallery, depending on the location and event covered. Team packages are also available at a discounted rate of $6–$13 per player.

I also create high-resolution sports graphics starting at $25 for one graphic and up to $60 for a three-graphic bundle (save 20%). Select athletic events may receive graphics at a discounted price or even free of charge.
